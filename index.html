About Me

Hi! Welcome to my portfolio.

I'm incredibly excited about taking this course and learning more about web development and I hope to use this space to show off the skills I gain as I progress through the next 24 weeks of classes.

I'm originally from a small town of about 2,500 people in rural Nebraska. I went to school at the University of Nebraska at Kearney (Go Lopes) where I graduated in 2009 with bachelor’s degrees in psychology and sociology. In 2011 I completed my master’s degree and graduated with a M.S.Ed. in Student Affairs.

In 2014 I moved to Atlanta and began working at Georgia State University as an Academic Counselor.

While I have enjoyed my work in higher education, I am looking forward to the challenges that lie ahead as I take this BootCamp course and I look forward to changing careers in the future with my new skills.
